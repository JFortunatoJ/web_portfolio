Hey There!

My name is João Fortunato Jr, I am a game developer and I live in Brazil. I decided in mid-2012 that I would turn my passion for games into my profession so I started to study and develop simple projects using Unity Engine. In 2014 I graduated from high school, in 2015 in technical education in computer science, where I had my first solid contact with programming, and in 2016 i started my Digital Games degree at FACENS, graduating in 2018 when I started working as a unity developer. I love learning new things and challenges motivate me, I always try to develop the projects in which I participate in the best possible way, worrying not only about the development but also the management.


Below you can check the projects in which I participated in the development, both professional and personal, as well as tutorial videos that I publish on youtube, not only to share knowledge but also as a source of learning about new subjects :D

Feel free to send a message if you want to know more about the projects or have any questions.
